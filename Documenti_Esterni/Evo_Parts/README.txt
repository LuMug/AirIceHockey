# Summary

The new <a href="http://www.jjrobots.com/air-hockey-robot-evo/"> Air Hockey Robot EVO</a>. Perfect to learn about  <strong>Robotics, visual recognition,behavioural robotics</strong> (and <strong>have fun!</strong>)

Based on our first Air Hockey Robot, this new Air Hockey Robot EVO is much easier to set-up and deploy. Now, controlled by your <strong>own smartphone</strong> using the <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.jjrobots.ahr_evo_mobile"> freely available APP (the APP will let you control the robot manually too) </a>

A challenging robot, perfect to have fun and learn robotics at the same time

This EVO version is faster, easily modifiable and shares the electronics and ancillary elements with others jjRobots like the <a href="http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1590702"> iBoardbot </a>,<a href="http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1069256"> B-robot EVO,</a> <a href="http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:1683764"> Sphere-O-bot,</a> (and more to come!)

We have based this new design on the standard parts of a typical 3D printer, such as the <a href="https://jjrobots.com/product/stepper-motor-nema17/">NEMA 17 stepper motors</a>, the <a href="https://jjrobots.com/product/b-robot-electronic-brain-shield-v2-0/">jjRobots Brain Shield</a>,belts, bearings and rods.

We have opted to use a <strong>medium-sized</strong> air hockey table with dimensions of 80 x 42 cm (31.5 x 16.5 in) and a two-motor X-Y movement design (H-bot), with both motors working at the same time, moving the robot up/down and left/right. Simple but ...cool

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Ultimaker
Printer: Ultimaker 2
Resolution: 0.20
Infill: 35

Notes: 
PLA works perfectly. 

NOTE: Do not use BLUE or GREEN to print the 3D parts.
The reason: the robot is always looking for BLUE and GREEN features on the playing court in order to detect the PUCK and the PUSHER.
NOTE: Print the PUCK with support. Then, remove it.
Recommended Filament colours : BLACK/RED or WHITE
